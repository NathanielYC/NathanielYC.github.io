About Me

Technology has always been the driving force of my passions since I built my first PC around age 13. Computer mechanics and electronics have enriched my life with amusement and knowledge for as long as I can remember. I took classes throughout my college journey to expand upon my knowledge, leading me to my standpoint today. I see myself working in the videogame industry, a field where I am able to combine my love of technology, innovation, and artistic creativity into one.

My educational endeavors have a wide range, including music production, musical performance, computer mechanics, computer coding, 3D rendering, video game animation, and gaming principles. I plan to utilize these experiences to create games with solid mechanics, beautiful storylines, and meticulous environments.
